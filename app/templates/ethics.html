Ethics of Neuroengineering

Your scientists were so preoccupied with whether or not they could, they didn't stop to think if they should.

Neuroengineering may very well be the most ethically controversial topic of the next century. It is typically agreed upon that innovating methods of treating disease is a good thing to do, but what about enhancing the physical and cognitive functions of one who is well?
